## About

I work across technology and natural capital, bridging gaps between people, systems, and contexts.

As a Bridge Engineer, I support offshore development and project execution by translating not only language but also intent, structure, and background.

In parallel, through "えるあの葉", I explore natural materials and local ecosystems, working with *kozo* (楮) and regional resources to create sustainable value.

These two domains may appear different, but they share a common foundation:
**understanding context and connecting what is divided.**

* Kozo cultivation and research
* Product and experience design using natural materials
